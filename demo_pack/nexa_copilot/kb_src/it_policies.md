# NexaCorp IT Policies (Employee-Facing Summary)

## Acceptable Use

Employees must use NexaCorp systems for business purposes. Personal use is limited per NXPOL-2024-AUP.

## Access Requests

Staging and production access require manager approval via the Access Hub. Break-glass emergency access exists but requires dual authorization — details are in the internal wiki (restricted).

## Data Handling

Customer and employee PII must not be exported in bulk. Individual record lookups for legitimate support tasks are permitted with ticket reference.

## Security Reporting

Report suspected phishing or credential leaks to security@nexacorp.internal immediately.
