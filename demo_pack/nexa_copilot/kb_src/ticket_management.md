# NexaCorp Ticket Management

## Ticket Format

Incident tickets use the format INC-##### (e.g., INC-48291).

## Status Values

- **New** — Ticket created, awaiting triage
- **In Progress** — Assigned to an engineer
- **Pending** — Waiting on user or vendor response
- **Resolved** — Fix applied, awaiting confirmation
- **Closed** — Confirmed resolved by requester

## How to Check Status

Employees can view ticket status in the IT self-service portal under "My Requests" or ask Nexa Copilot for a summary.

## SLA Targets

- P1 (Critical): 4 hours
- P2 (High): 8 business hours
- P3 (Normal): 2 business days
